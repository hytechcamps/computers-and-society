Avatar Icebreaker

Follow the instructions below to create an avatar, and share it with the class.

  1. Go to this website to start: https://readyplayer.me/avatar
  2. Go through the creation process - try to make yourself!
    • Note: It is not recommended to take/upload a picture of yourself
  3. When you're done customizing, click the "ENTER HUB →" button in the upper right
  4. Once it loads, click the "Share" button
  5. In the pop-up that appears, click the "Copy Image URL" button
  6. Now, open up this form: https://forms.gle/nrSLJzVycNWxDBBg9
  7. Select your Room number, enter your full name, and then paste in the link to your created avatar!

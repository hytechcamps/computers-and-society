A.I. Icebreaker

Follow the instructions below to create an A.I-generated profile picture, and share it with the class.

  1. Go to this website to start: https://creator.nightcafe.studio/create/text-to-image
  2. Under Your text prompt, enter a physical description of yourself
    • Try to be as detailed as possible - feel free to describe yourself in an interesting setting or doing something you enjoy!
  3. Choose a style, choose an algorithm, and click the CREATE button at the bottom
  4. Scroll down a little bit to see the art start to render!
  5. Hover over the image on the right and click the VIEW CREATION button
  6. Highlight the URL in the browser address bar and copy it with CTRL+c
  7. Now, open up this form: https://forms.gle/wPr4V2xAKzLSPF3x9
  8. Select your room number, enter your full name, and then paste in the link to your created profile picture!
